About

Expert in Digital Transformation. I have a Ph.D. in Industrial Technologies and Telecommunications in Universidad Miguel Hernandez in Elche. I also performed my Computer Engineer degree & a Master in Digital Home Systems at Universidad de Alicante.

I have both relevant, education and experience, in different countries. During my Ph.D. I performed a six-month stay in Boston (MA) in Northeastern University and MIT in 2010, after finishing the Ph.D I worked for two years in UK at Santander Banking Group. After that I came back to Spain to Murcia to work in a Startup developing for Google Glass AR andVR, in which a had a lot of relation with Silicon Valley companies, specially with Google.

Since 2017 I work at European Intellectual Property Office (EUIPO), my main task has been to adapt EUIPO tools and solutions for European and non-European countries Intellectual Property Offices, such as Sweden, Denmark, Hungary, many in Latin America, Asia, Africa and others like Bosnia, Georgia, etc
